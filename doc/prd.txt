PRD — TripIT AI (Intelligent Tour & Travel Platform)
1. Objective

Build an AI-powered travel platform that allows users to discover destinations, generate personalized itineraries, and simulate bookings with transparent pricing — all in one flow.

The platform must be AI-first, fast, and demo-ready.

2. Problem

Users face:

Fragmented trip planning (multiple apps)

Generic recommendations

Hidden or unclear costs

Time-consuming itinerary creation

3. Solution

An AI-driven web app where users describe their trip in natural language and instantly receive:

A day-wise itinerary

Cost breakdown

Travel tips

Editable plan

Booking simulation

5. AI Design
Model

AI Responsibilities

Generate realistic itineraries

Stay within budget

Produce structured JSON output

AI Prompt (System)
You are a professional travel planner.
Generate realistic, budget-aware, day-wise itineraries.
Include cost estimates and practical travel tips.
Return structured JSON only.
